Supplemental_Information:
Private Well Tracking System Wells Data has four tables that provide additional data: PWTS_contruction provides additional information about well construction. It can be linked (one-to-many relationship) to the well point shape file using the wellnmbr. PWTS_litho consists of lithologic information. It can be linked (one-to-many relationship) to the well point shape file using the wellnmbr. PWTS_production contains pumping information collected from initial capacity testing. It has a one-to-many relation ship with the shapefile and is related throught the wellnmbr field. PWTS_water_quality (limited availability) contains basic water quality information (bacteria and nitrate as nitrogen). It has a one-to-many relation ship with the shapefile and is related throught the wellnmbr field.
Abstract:
Point locations of wells entered into the Iowa DNR Private Well Tracking System. Includes private well permits, well test registration, abandoned well registration, and well renovation registration administered by the grants-to-counties program.

Data_Quality_Information:
Positional_Accuracy:
Horizontal_Positional_Accuracy:
Horizontal_Positional_Accuracy_Report:
User provides location from an interactive map service that provides georeferenced 7.5' quadrangle or 2002 aerial photography. Location may not be collected until the map scale is 1 pixel/meter. The user centers the location on the view then selects "Capture location" to enter the coordinates for the well. The nomimal accuracy is estimated to be +/- 22m. No validity checking against PLSS locations, for example, is performed. The user may enter GPS readings as an alternative method of coordinate capture. In this case the accuracy is assumed to be +/- 15 to 25 meters. If the latter method is used, no accuracy is recorded by the database. The datum is assumed to be NAD83.

Overview_Description:
Entity_and_Attribute_Overview:
WELLNMBR: Unique well identifier carried over from Private Well Tracking System database PMTNMBR: Private well permit number, not required TWP: Public Land Survey System Tier number, calculated by PWTS from the Latitude/Longitude RGE: Public Land Survey System Range number, calculated by PWTS from the Latitude/Longitude EW: Public Land Survey System Range direction, calculated by PWTS from the Latitude/Longitude SEC: Public Land Survey System Section number, calculated by PWTS from the Latitude/Longitude QQQQQ: Public Land Survey System quarter sections, in largest to smallest order, calculated by PWTS from the Latitude/Longitude ELEV: Elevation of land surface, entered by PWTS user or calculated from statewide Digital Elevation Model ELEV_ACC: Accuracy of ELEV WELL_DEPTH: Total depth of well WTR_DEPTH: Depth to water in well; -99 if not entered LATITUDE: Latitude of well, collected from user on PWTS website (NAD83) LONGITUDE: Longitude of well, collected from user on PWTS website (NAD83) DTE_DONE: Completion date of well (may be blank if not known) COUNTY: The county where the well is located DRL_NAME: Individual listed on permit as well driller; may be unknown for pre-existing wells OWNER_NAME: Owner/tenant at time of transaction with Grants-to-Counties CNSTR_MTHD: Method used to contruct the well; may be unknown or left blank FM_RMK: General remarks about the well or data entered by the user WELL_USE: Primary and secondary use for the well STATUS: Status of the well. May be ACTIVE/RETIRED/PLUGGED. RETIRED refers to permitted wells for which no data is entered within one year of issuance. PLUGGED wells are abandoned by a certified well contractor according to state guidelines. ACTIVE is everything else. HT_PMP_NO: The number of wells in a heat pump installation. Each heat pump installation is treated as a single well. PERMIT: If "X", the well is a permitted private well; used to document the origin of the record in PWTS. PLUGGING: If "X", the well has been abandoned under the Grants-to-Counties program; used to document the origin of the record in PWTS. RENOVATE: If "X", the well was renovated under the Grants-to-Counties program: used to document the origin of the record in PWTS. TEST: If "X", the well was registered for testing (NO3 and bacteria) under the Grants-to-Counties program: used to document the origin of the record in PWTS. XCOORD: X coordinate (Easting) UTM Zone 15, NAD83 YCOORD: Y coordinate (Northing) UTM Zone 15, NAD83 LITHOLOGY: T/F; if true, one or more records are in the litho.dbf table
