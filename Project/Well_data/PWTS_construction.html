Description:
Abstract:
Point locations of wells entered into the Iowa DNR Private Well Tracking System. Includes private well permits, well test registration, abandoned well registration, and well renovation registration administered by the grants-to-counties program.
Purpose:
This digital, geographically referenced data set was developed by the Iowa Department of Natural Resources to carry out agency responsibilities related to management, protection, and development of Iowa's natural resources.
Supplemental_Information:
Private Well Tracking System Wells Data has four tables that provide additional data: PWTS_contruction provides additional information about well construction. It can be linked (one-to-many relationship) to the well point shape file using the wellnmbr. PWTS_lithology consists of lithologic information. It can be linked (one-to-many relationship) to the well point shape file using the wellnmbr and order with the from column. PWTS_production contains pumping information collected from initial capacity testing. It has a one-to-many relation ship with the shapefile and is related throught the wellnmbr field. PWTS_water_quality) contains basic water quality information (bacteria and nitrate as nitrogen). It has a one-to-many relationship with the shapefile and is related throught the wellnmbr field.
